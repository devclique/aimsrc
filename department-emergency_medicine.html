DEPARTMENT OF EMERGENCY MEDICINE

Welcome to the Emergency Department (ED) at our prestigious Akash institute of medical college and Hospital.

The Emergency Department serves as the front-line of our institution's commitment to the health and well-being of our community. Our dedicated team of highly skilled healthcare professionals, including doctors, nurses, technicians, and support staff, works tirelessly around the clock to deliver prompt and comprehensive care to patients who require immediate medical attention.

At the heart of our department's mission is the provision of rapid assessment, diagnosis, and treatment for patients presenting with a wide range of medical emergencies. From critical trauma cases, resuscitation, adult and paediatric emergencies and acute illnesses, we are equipped to handle a diverse array of conditions and ensure that every patient receives the timely and appropriate care they need.

Our department is designed to provide a safe and efficient environment where patients receive specialized emergency care. We have state-of-the-art facilities, including well-equipped resuscitation rooms, trauma bays, diagnostic imaging capabilities, and a dedicated triage area to prioritize patients based on the severity of their conditions. This enables us to swiftly identify life-threatening situations and initiate immediate interventions.

In addition to our commitment to acute care, our Emergency Department also emphasizes compassionate patient-centered care. We understand that emergencies can be distressing, and our team strives to provide comfort and support to patients and their families throughout their ED experience. We prioritize clear communication, respect, and empathy to ensure that patients feel heard, understood, and cared for during their time with us.

As an academic medical institution, our Emergency Department serves as a valuable training ground for future healthcare professionals. We actively engage in medical education, research, and innovation to advance the field of emergency medicine. Our staff is involved in teaching and mentoring medical students, residents, and fellows, fostering a culture of continuous learning and professional growth.

Education and Research:

Undergraduate and Postgraduate courses:

We are recognised by NMC for undergraduate and post-graduate education and paramedical courses in emergency medicine, nurturing many numbers of students to excel every year.

Continuing Medical Education:

We encourage and do regular academic activities, subject seminars, guest lectures, training programs. We are equipped with seminar hall, class room, skill lab and library.

Our Team

Doctors

Our team of highly skilled physicians is dedicated to delivering expert care to every patient.

Nurses

Our compassionate and experienced nursing staff plays a vital role in caring for patients and supporting their families.

Technicians

Our team of knowledgeable technicians ensures that our facilities and equipment are functioning at the highest level of safety and efficiency.

Support Staff

Our administrative and support staff is committed to providing a welcoming and organized environment where patients can receive the care they need.

Our Mission and Values

Mission

We are committed to delivering the highest quality emergency medical care to our community, prioritizing rapid assessment, diagnosis, and treatment for acute and critical conditions.

Values

We value patient-centered care, empathy, clear communication, and respect, and strive to create a compassionate and supportive environment for our patients and their families.

Goals

Our goals are to continuously improve the quality of our care, foster a culture of learning and professional development, and advance the field of emergency medicine through research and innovation.

Our Facilities

Resuscitation Rooms

Our specialized resuscitation rooms provide a controlled environment for emergency interventions and care.

Trauma Bays

Our spacious and well-equipped trauma bays are designed to accommodate patients with critical injuries and conditions, enabling us to provide rapid assessment and treatment.

Diagnostic Imaging

Our on-site diagnostic imaging facilities include x-ray, ultrasound, and CT capabilities, which help us to accurately diagnose conditions and develop effective treatment plans.

Our Triage System

  • Triage Assessment

    Upon arrival, our nursing staff conducts a quick assessment to assign each patient a triage category that prioritizes care based on the severity of their condition.

  • Rapid Evaluation

    Patients then receive a rapid evaluation to initiate diagnostic testing and treatment as quickly as possible.

  • Expert Care

    Our team of physicians and healthcare professionals utilize their expertise and experience to provide specialized and comprehensive emergency medical care.

Our Education and Training Programs

Undergraduate Medical Education

Our institution is a recognized center for undergraduate education in emergency medicine, empowering the next generation of medical professionals.

Continuing Medical Education

We prioritize the continuing education and professional development of our team, providing opportunities for regular academic activities, subject seminars, guest lectures, and training programs.

Conferences and Seminars

We also organize conferences and seminars to facilitate the sharing of new research, ideas, and best practices in emergency medicine.

Our Research and Innovation

  • Medical Research

    We actively engage in medical research to advance our understanding of emergency medicine, utilizing the latest discoveries to improve our clinical practice.

  • Innovative Technologies

    We are committed to innovation, continuously seeking new ways to improve the quality, efficiency, and effectiveness of our emergency medical care.

  • Clinical Trials

    We also conduct clinical trials to explore new treatments and therapies, contributing to the advancement of medical science.

Our Location

Akash Institute of Medical College and Hospital is located on the Prassanahalli main road in Devanahalli, Bengaluru. Our Emergency Department is open around the clock to serve the members of our community who require immediate medical attention.
